Haiku Bot is a Lego Mindstorm robot that writes!

Concept

The idea is simple: create a robot, based off of the Lego Mindstorm brick, that can compose and write haikus. For those that don't know, a haiku is a Japanese three-line poem consisting of five, seven and five syllables each. The long-term goal was to create a robot skilled enough with a pen to write entire sentences.
